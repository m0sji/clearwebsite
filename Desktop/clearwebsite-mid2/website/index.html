01 How do you ensure models stay accurate in production? ›

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Answers to common questions about timelines, data requirements and operations.

? What data do you need to start? ›
Minimal start requires a sample of historical data, a clear target (business KPI) and examples of expected outputs. We run a data readiness check during discovery.
? How do you measure project success? ›
Projects have defined success metrics (e.g., accuracy uplift, cost savings, conversion increase). We track both technical and business KPIs through launch and beyond.
? Can you integrate with our existing systems? ›
Yes — we build adapters and APIs to integrate models into your stack, and provide deployment patterns compatible with cloud or on-premise environments.
? What ongoing maintenance is required? ›
Maintenance can include monitoring, retraining schedules, data labeling, and periodic reviews. We offer managed plans for continuous operation.
? How do you handle model explainability and bias? ›
We run explainability checks, fairness audits and provide design changes or constraints to mitigate bias. Findings and mitigations are documented for stakeholders.
